Before You Start

This page is for reporting bugs in implementations of Micro-Api, as distributed on the New-Justice-League page and the HNGi7 page. This site is not for development Support, but for the developers to contribute and be involved with our Micro-Api's engineers in fixing and improving our API.

Do not submit a bug if you need technical support or have a question about how to use a product. Instead please review the community free support options.

Refer to the Bug Submission FAQ's to answer questions you have related to the bug database and the bug Submission Process.
